Welcome to Arora’s Modern Indian Bistro

At Arora’s Modern Indian Bistro, we proudly serve authentic Indian cuisine inspired by traditional recipes and bold spices. Located in Freehold Township, NJ, our restaurant offers a warm, welcoming atmosphere perfect for family dining, celebrations, and special occasions.

From rich curries to freshly baked naan from our tandoor, every dish is prepared with passion and care.
